We want to tell you, according to the latest analysis, more than 50% of sites published on the Internet have problems with indexing, design, and effectiveness, although it is true that companies use their websites to maintain contact with their customers, it is a very useful tool to give themselves Meet and get new clients for both established companies and new ventures.

MKM web consultants want to become a strategic partner of web consulting services for all companies that have problems with their websites and that do not achieve goals, we have trained personnel and the necessary tools to take your company to compete like the big ones.

If you are interested in starting digital marketing for your company, keep in mind that the traffic directed to your website must be measurable and all the interactions that take place within your site must provide important information to improve your offer of services or products.

WEB CONSULTING (page 2)

WHO WE ARE

We are a group of web technology specialists with extensive experience in building, monitoring, and optimizing websites. We want your company to turn your website into a powerful digital marketing tool and open up new opportunities for growth and interaction with your customers.

WHAT WE DO

We carry out web consulting processes at different levels to repair, rebuild, update or build high-quality competitive websites within market niches and that provides companies with the possibility of growth through the website as a commercial tool.

HOW WE DO IT 

We start with a basic evaluation that can give the site owner an idea of ​​the main weaknesses or aspects to improve on their site, then through software tools, we make deeper analyzes of the site as a whole and its performance on the network We obtain SEO indicators monitoring each aspect of the web in detail, then we generate the results together with an action plan with specific recommendations to improve the site, becoming strategic allies of our clients.

BUSSINES

EXPERTS
